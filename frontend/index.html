Why should you have a cat?

Having a cat around you can actually trigger the release of calming chemicals in your body which lower your stress and anxiety leves. A cat has the ability to both calm your nervous system and provide an immediate outlet for fun and play. Although cats are independent animals who like to scavenge and explore on their own terms, they are also very affectionate with their owners and people they trust.
